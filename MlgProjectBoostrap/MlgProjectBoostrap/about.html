Our Story

MJG Renovations LLC was built on the belief that every home deserves quality craftsmanship. What started as a small local renovation service has grown into a trusted contractor serving metro Atlanta and surrounding areas.

We take pride in delivering results that not only look great—but last. Whether it’s kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, or whole-home transformations, we approach every project with care, precision, and the client’s vision in mind.
